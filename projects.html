Sparra

Sparra is a comprehensive application designed to connect combat sports athletes with training partners. The platform enables users to create personalized profiles showcasing their stats, allowing them to find compatible training partners.

Key Features

  • Profile Creation: Users can create profiles showcasing their stats, including height, weight, age, and experience level.
  • Training Partner Matching: Users can find compatible training partners based on their stats and preferences.
  • Event Promotion: Users can share and discover upcoming combat sports events.
